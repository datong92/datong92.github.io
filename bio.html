Biography

About Myself

I was a PhD Candidate in Mechanical Engineering at UC Berkeley, working with Professor Claire J. Tomlin in the Hybrid Systems Laboratory. Also, I was affiliated with the Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research Lab (BAIR). I am interested in modeling and control of Cyber-Physical Systems using Applied Machine Learning and Statistical Learning. Please take a look at my projects and publications that are at the intersection of control theory, computer science, and economics, with applications to electricity markets.

Education

I have obtained the following academic degrees:

  • PhD in Mechanical Engineering, UC Berkeley, December 2018

  • MA in Mathematics, UC Berkeley, May 2018

  • MS in Mechanical Engineering, UC Berkeley, May 2017

  • MS in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences (EECS), UC Berkeley, May 2016

  • BS in Mechanical Engineering, Technische Universität München (Germany), February 2014

During the Fall 2016 semester I was a visiting student in the Laboratory for Information and Decision Systems (LIDS) at MIT, supervised by Prof. Munther A. Dahleh and Dr. Mardavij Roozbehani.

Work Experience

  • In the summer of 2018, I worked at Facebook's News Feed Discovery Team as a Software Engineer Intern (Machine Learning focus).

  • In the summer of 2015, I worked as a Consulting Intern for Siemens AG in Munich, Germany.
