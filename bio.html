About Myself

I am currently an Applied Scientist Manager and Tech Lead at Amazon Advertising, having previously worked at Uber and Meta in roles focused on Applied Science and ML Engineering. Before that, I completed by PhD at UC Berkeley, working with Professor Claire J. Tomlin in the Hybrid Systems Laboratory and being part of the Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research Lab (BAIR).

My work in industry broadly revolves around the application of Machine Learning, statistical methods, and economics in large-scale distributed systems across diverse sectors such as search and recommendation systems, advertising, e-commerce, and transportation.
